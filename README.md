# South India Shopping Store – Sales Analysis (2022)

## Project Overview
This project analyzes the **South India Shopping Store dataset (2022)** to uncover insights about:
- Sales trends
- Customer behavior
- Regional performance
- Sales channels

The analysis is performed using **Excel (Pivot Tables & Dashboard)** to answer key business questions and provide actionable insights.

---

## 🎯 Problem Statement
- How do monthly sales and orders vary throughout the year?
- Which gender contributes more to sales?
- Which states generate the highest revenue?
- Which age group contributes the most?
- Which sales channels perform best?
- How do gender and age together influence purchasing?

---

## 📁 Dataset Description
The dataset contains:
- **Order ID** – Unique identifier  
- **Month** – Order time  
- **Amount** – Revenue  
- **Gender** – Customer gender  
- **Age Group** – Customer category  
- **Channel** – Platform (Amazon, Flipkart, etc.)  
- **State** – Customer location  

---

## 📈 Key Insights

### 📅 Monthly Sales Trend
- Highest sales in **March**
- Strong performance in **Q1**
- Gradual decline after mid-year

---

### Gender-Based Analysis
- Women contribute **majority of sales**
- Indicates strong opportunity for targeted marketing

---

### 📦 Order Status
- **90%+ orders successfully delivered**
- Very low cancellations/returns

---

###  State-wise Performance
Top states:
- Maharashtra  
- Karnataka  
- Uttar Pradesh  

---

### 🛍️ Sales Channels
Top platforms:
- Amazon (~35%)
- Flipkart (~22%)
- Myntra (~23%)
